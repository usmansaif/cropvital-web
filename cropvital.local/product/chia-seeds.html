Chia Seeds

Chia seeds are tiny black and white seeds, valued worldwide as a nutrient-dense superfood. They are naturally gluten-free, high in omega-3 fatty acids, dietary fiber, and plant-based protein. In Pakistan, they are popularly used in summer drinks, smoothies, and breakfast bowls.

Key Benefits

  • Supports heart health with plant-based omega-3 (ALA).
  • High in fiber, helping with digestion and weight management.
  • Provides plant protein for muscle strength and energy.
  • Rich in calcium and magnesium for strong bones.
  • Packed with antioxidants to fight oxidative stress.

How to Use

You can enjoy chia seeds in multiple ways:

  • Soak 1–2 tablespoons in water or milk for 15–30 minutes before use.
  • Add to smoothies, yogurt, or porridge.
  • Sprinkle over fruit bowls, salads, or cereals.
  • Use as a vegan egg substitute in baking (1 tbsp chia + 3 tbsp water).
  • Mix into refreshing drinks (like falooda or summer coolers).

Storage Tips

  • Keep in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
  • Refrigerate after opening for longer freshness.
  • Avoid moisture — only soak the portion you need immediately.
