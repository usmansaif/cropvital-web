Watermelon Seeds

Watermelon seeds are often overlooked, but when dried and roasted, they become a nutrient-packed snack loaded with protein, healthy fats, and minerals.

Benefits

  • High in protein, supporting muscle health and repair.
  • Rich in magnesium and zinc for energy and immunity.
  • Contains healthy fats that support heart health.

Usage

Roast watermelon seeds for a crunchy snack, blend into smoothies, or use as a topping for cereals and salads.

Storage Tips

Store roasted watermelon seeds in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Consume within a few weeks for best freshness.
