Desi Ghee

Desi ghee is a traditional clarified butter made from high-quality milk. It has been a staple in South Asian kitchens for centuries and is renowned for its rich flavor, aroma, and nutritional benefits. Our premium desi ghee is pure, natural, and free from additives or preservatives.

Key Benefits

  • Rich source of healthy fats for energy and brain function.
  • Supports when consumed in moderation.
  • Contains fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E, and K for overall wellness.
  • Boosts immunity and enhances metabolism.
  • Perfect for cooking, frying, and baking without losing nutrients.

How to Use

Desi ghee can be used in various ways to enrich your meals and health:

  • Spread on warm bread or chapati for natural flavor.
  • Use in cooking, frying, or sautéing instead of oil.
  • Add to milk, dal, or khichdi for enhanced nutrition.
  • Incorporate in desserts, halwa, or sweet dishes for rich taste.

Storage Tips

  • Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
  • Avoid direct sunlight to maintain flavor and color.
  • Refrigeration is optional, but recommended for long-term storage.
