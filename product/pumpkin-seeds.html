Pumpkin Seeds (Kaddu Ke Beej)

Pumpkin seeds are nutrient-rich seeds packed with magnesium, zinc, protein, and healthy fats. They are a powerhouse for boosting immunity, improving sleep, and supporting heart health.

Key Benefits

  • Rich source of magnesium for strong bones and muscles.
  • Improves sleep due to tryptophan content.
  • Boosts immunity with zinc and antioxidants.
  • Supports heart and prostate health.
  • Good plant-based protein source.

How to Use

  • Snack roasted pumpkin seeds on their own.
  • Add to salads, smoothies, and granola.
  • Mix in baked goods for extra crunch.

Storage Tips

  • Store in airtight jars to maintain crunchiness.
  • Refrigerate after opening for longer shelf life.
